Welcome!


Welcome to our website! Comprised of a group of talented young men and women, Anokha seeks to spread Indian and American culture and music to the community by performing popular Indian film and pop songs, in an array of languages including Hindi, Tamil and Punjabi, as well as by performing English pop, rock, R&B, and alternative music.
